Test data are in \anti_occlusion_test. 
The sub-directory is named as "Lr-BN1-BP1-TN1-TP1-BN2-BP2-TN2-TP2",
where
Lr - 0r means common LIDAR, i.e. R is almost 0.
     1r means long-arm LIDAR.
BN1, BN2 - number of barrier, 8 or 9
BP1, BP2 - position of barrier (mm)
TN1, TN2 - number of target, 1, 3 or 10
TP1, TP2 - position of target (mm)
